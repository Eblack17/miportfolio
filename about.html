My Design Journey

My design journey began at the University of Design Arts where I developed a strong foundation in traditional design principles. Since then, I've worked with agencies and direct clients across various industries, from tech startups to established retail brands.

I believe in the power of thoughtful design to solve problems, tell stories, and create meaningful connections. My approach combines strategic thinking with aesthetic sensibility to deliver results that are both beautiful and effective.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring photography, reading about design trends, or hiking in the mountains. These activities fuel my creativity and provide fresh perspectives that I bring back to my design work.
